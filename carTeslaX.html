TESLA MODEL X

Best in Class Storage

The all-electric powertrain in Model X allows for tremendous cargo capacity by eliminating the room needed for an engine, radiators, exhausts and other mechanical vehicle equipment. This extra space allows for best in class storage, and provides the capacity for a second trunk, located in the front.

Seating for up to Seven

Designed to maximize passenger comfort, Model X has five, six and seven seat configuration options to suit the specific needs of you and your family. Second and third row seats can fold flat when not in use, allowing for more interior cargo space.
